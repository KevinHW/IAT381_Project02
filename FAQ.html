FAQ

How do I take care of a bonsai tree?
Water the bonsai tree as often as needed, preferably in the early morning, and give the tree regular weekly trims. Since our bonsai trees come with a pot and soil, there is no need to look anything else.

Can I feed my bonsai tree fertilizer to make it a big tree?
Bonsai trees can grow like normal trees, given sufficient breathing room. We recommend leaving the tree outside.

Where do bonsai trees originate from?
Bonsai trees originated from Japan as a form of art.

Do you ship to international addresses?
Unfortunately due the to nature of our delicate bonsai trees, we do not ship internationally.
