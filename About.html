What started out as a hobby of bonsai tree-collecting turned out to be one of Vancouver's most talked about local businesses. Mini Trees is just what we have dreamed of. 2009 was when the idea of opening up a bonsai tree store sparked. June and I took notice of Vancouver's lack of these miniature trees in the plant market, so we thought why not? Fast forward to 2012: The grand opening of Mini Trees! Thank you for all the love and support!
