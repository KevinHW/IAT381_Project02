What is Bonsai?

Bonsai is the ancient Japanese art form of carefully directing the growth of miniature trees.

It requires creativity, contemplation and a steady, careful hand to achieve the minimal aesthetic that bonsai trees bring to a home.

The goal of the artist is to keep the bonsai tree asymmetrical, proportional to a large tree and to leave as little trace of their craft as possible.
